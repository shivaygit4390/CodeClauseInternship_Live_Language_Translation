Remarks
  • The translator may produce inappropriate outputs for some inputs. This is because we have used a free API, and there are certain limitations.
  • Please try to avoid unnecessary spaces, especially for single-word inputs.
  • To use text-to-speech in languages other than English, please ensure that you have enabled the respective language in your browser and device.
  • Due to the limitations of the API, only 5000 characters can be translated in one day.
  • Please allow some time after entering your input before expecting a response. The response time may be influenced and could take a few moments depending on the internet speed and the length of the text.
